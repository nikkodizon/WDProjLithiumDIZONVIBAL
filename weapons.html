WEAPONS IN VALORANT

Weapons in VALORANT are the primary tools agents use to secure eliminations and win rounds. Each weapon category offers a different balance of damage, accuracy, fire rate, and cost, rewarding players who understand when and how to use them. From precise rifles and powerful sniper rifles to close-range shotguns and sidearms, weapon choice plays a major role in shaping a team’s strategy.

Guns have limited ammo and come with three magazines’ worth of reserve ammunition. Damage varies depending on the weapon, the body part hit, and the distance to the target. Weapon accuracy is affected by spread and movement; most guns have first-shot spread, and moving while shooting generally adds accuracy penalties.

Weapons are bought with credits during the pre-round. Every agent starts with a free Melee weapon and a Classic sidearm. Purchased weapons are kept until death and can be picked up from the ground.
