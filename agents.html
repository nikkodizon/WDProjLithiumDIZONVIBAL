INFORMATION ON AGENTS IN VALORANT

Agents are the playable characters in VALORANT, each with unique abilities that define their role in a match.

Story & Lore

Story-wise, agents are members of the VALORANT Protocol, a secret organization created to deal with threats involving radianite. Each agent is assigned a protocol number based on the order in which they officially joined the organization, and these numbers are often referenced in voice lines, player cards, and official media. Agents come from different countries around the world and have their own backgrounds, personalities, and motivations. Some agents are Radiants, meaning they gained supernatural powers through radianite, while others rely on advanced technology, training, or weapons. Together, they work to stop global threats linked to radianite and mirror agents from alternate realities.

Gameplay

Gameplay-wise, agents each have a unique set of abilities that affect how they are played and how they support their team. Every agent has one signature ability, which can recharge over time or through actions, and two other normal abilities that must be bought using credits during the Buy Phase. In addition, each agent has an ultimate ability, which is a powerful skill that is charged by getting kills, planting or defusing the spike, or collecting ultimate orbs around the map. Some agents also have passive abilities, which are always active and do not need to be equipped or purchased.

Agents are designed to fill specific roles such as attacking, defending, gathering information, or controlling areas of the map. Choosing the right agent can greatly influence how a round is played and the overall team strategy. Agents are divided into four main roles: Duelists, Initiators, Controllers, and Sentinels, each with a clear purpose in a match.

VALORANT originally launched with 10 agents, and RIOT continues to expand the roster by releasing a new agent roughly every episode, keeping the game fresh and introducing new playstyles over time.

Fun Fact: Agent 08 is the only missing protocol number in VALORANT, and their identity has never been officially revealed. Riot Games has stated that Agent 08 will be addressed in the future, leading many players to believe they may appear later in the story or as a future agent connected to major lore events.

DUELISTS

Duelists are designed to take fights and create space for their team. A key feature of Duelists is that their signature ability can be recharged by getting two kills in a round, encouraging aggressive play and rewarding players who successfully engage the enemy. INITIATORS

Initiators are agents who focus on setting up their team for success by gathering information and disrupting enemy positions. Their abilities help clear angles, reveal enemy locations, or force opponents to move, making it easier for teammates to take space and secure sites. Many Initiators have a signature ability that refreshes after a cooldown, allowing them to be used multiple times in a round if timed well. Rather than leading with pure aggression, Initiators play a key role in enabling pushes, executes, and retakes through smart utility usage and teamwork. CONTROLLERS

Controllers are agents who specialize in shaping the battlefield by controlling vision and space. They use smokes, walls, and area-denial abilities to block sightlines, cut off enemy movement, and help their team safely take or defend sites. Usually, a Controller’s smoke ability can recharge after a certain amount of time, allowing them to place smokes multiple times in a round. This makes Controllers crucial for controlling the pace of the game and supporting their team throughout the round. There are currently 6 playable controllers as of 12/16/2025

SENTINELS

Sentinels are agents focused on defense, area control, and protecting their teammates. They specialize in locking down sites, watching flanks, and delaying enemies with traps, slows, and information-gathering utility. Their abilities often stay active until destroyed or triggered, making them reliable throughout the round. Sentinels are essential for maintaining map control and team safety on both attack and defense. There are currently 7 playable Sentinels as of 12/16/2025.

PHOENIX

DUELIST

Description

Phoenix is Agent 09 in VALORANT and one of the original Duelists available since the Closed Beta. His real name is Jamie Adeyemi, a Radiant from the Peckham district of London, England. He attended a performing arts school growing up but was later suspended. Phoenix was recruited into the VALORANT Protocol and quickly became involved in its operations. Early in his time with the Protocol, much of the information was kept from him due to his recent recruitment. Because of this, Phoenix failed to stop a spike detonation in Venice while on a solo mission. The next day, during another attack in Rabat, he successfully prevented a detonation and came face-to-face with his Omega Earth counterpart for the first time. After this mission, Phoenix was finally informed about the larger threat posed by Omega Earth. This agent is Aamir’s personal favorite to use!

    • Blind Duration: 1.5s
    • Cost: 250 credits
  • BLAZE: EQUIP a flame wall. FIRE to create a line of flame that moves forward. HOLD FIRE to bend. Blocks vision and damages players.
    • 1 Charge
    • Duration: 8s
    • Damage: 30 dmg/s
    • Cost: 150 credits
  • HOT HANDS: EQUIP a fireball. FIRE to throw, explodes after set time or hitting ground. Creates lingering fire zone that damages enemies. ALT FIRE to lob.
    • 1 Charge
    • Duration: 3.25s
    • Damage: 60 dmg/s
    • Cost: 200 credits
    • *Fun Fact: Used to be signature ability.*
  • HEATING UP (Passive): Passively heal Phoenix instead of taking damage when standing in his own fire. Heals 50 HP total.
  • RUN IT BACK (Ultimate): INSTANTLY place a marker. While active, dying or timer expiring brings Phoenix back to marker with full health.
    • Cost: 6 Ultimate Points
