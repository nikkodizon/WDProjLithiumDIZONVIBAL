About VBTC

Valorant: Beyond the Crosshair is an interactive guide designed to help players of all skill levels improve their gameplay. It offers detailed insights into agents, maps, weapons, and strategies — everything a player needs to understand the game in depth.


The goal of the website is to guide players from beginners to pros through engaging visuals, organized layouts, and interactive features that enhance understanding and gameplay. Whether you are looking to master a new agent or learn map callouts, VBTC is your tactical companion.
