INFORMATION ON MAPS IN VALORANT

Each match of VALORANT takes place on a map. In each game, the map is randomly chosen from the current map pool. Four maps were available at launch, and annually a new standard map is released. Overall, there are currently 12 playable maps.

Map rotation, which limits the number of playable maps at a time, was introduced in Episode 05 with the release of Pearl, VALORANT’s eighth map. The map pool is kept at seven to balance variety without overwhelming players, while allowing deeper mastery of each map. Rotations apply to Competitive, Premier, and Deathmatch, with decisions based on player sentiment, time since release, planned updates, and strategic diversity.

The maps currently available in these game modes are Abyss, Bind, Corrode, Haven, Pearl, Split, and Sunset, while the maps that are currently unavailable are Ascent, Breeze, Fracture, Icebox, and Lotus.

Fun Fact: Each map in VALORANT has a real-life location, with its coordinates shown on the loading screen when entering a game on that map.
IN ROTATION

Abyss

Abyss is the eleventh standard map released in VALORANT located in a deep underground facility in Norway. Abyss is the first map in VALORANT to have no outer boundaries and a death drop in Mid, making it possible in these areas for players to fall off the map to their death.

Important Callouts

  • A Site – A Main, A Tower, A Backsite, A Vent, A Lobby
  • Mid – Mid, Mid Catwalk, Mid Library, Mid Bottom, Mid Bend
  • B Site – B Main, B Lobby, B Tower, B Danger, B Site, B Nest

Pro Tips

  • Control Mid early: Mid is very open on Abyss, so gaining mid control gives faster rotations.
  • Use vertical utility on Drops and Towers.
  • Fake one site, hit the other: Long rotations make fakes very effective.
OUT OF ROTATION

Ascent

Ascent is the fourth standard map released in VALORANT located in Venice, Italy. It features mechanical doors on both spike sites that can be opened, closed, or destroyed. The map emphasizes mid control for split attacks. This map is also Aamir’s personal favorite.

Important Callouts

  • A Site – A Main, A Tree, A Heaven, A Hell, A Generator, A Dice
  • Mid – Mid Top, Mid Bottom, Mid Cubby, Mid Catwalk, Market
  • B Site – B Main, B Market, B Lane, B Backsite, B Site

Pro Tips

  • Abuse mid control: Taking Mid early lets your team split both A and B.
  • Play around doors and walls: Use doors to delay pushes or isolate enemies.
  • Use vertical angles on A Site: Clear Heaven and Hell properly.
IN ROTATION

Bind

Bind is located in Rabat, Morocco. It is unique because it has no mid area and instead uses two one-way teleporters that connect A Short to B Short and B Long to A Lobby. Teleporters make loud audio cues when used.

Important Callouts

  • A Site – A Short, A Bath (Showers), A Heaven, A Lamps (U-Hall), A Site
  • B Site – B Long, B Hookah, B Elbow, B Backsite, B Site

Pro Tips

  • Use teleporters to fake: Triggering a TP can force rotations.
  • Control Showers and Hookah: Key areas for executions.
  • Punish teleporter exits: Enemies are trapped in the exit room briefly.
OUT OF ROTATION

Breeze

Breeze is located in the Bermuda Triangle. It features long-range engagements and a unique A Hall area accessible by ropes and a chute. It has wide open sites that favor good aim.

Important Callouts

  • A Site – A Main, A Hall, A Bridge, A Site, A Backsite
  • Mid – Mid Doors, Mid Pillar, Mid Window, Mid Bottom
  • B Site – B Main, B Tunnel, B Elbow, B Backsite, B Site

Pro Tips

  • Take long-range fights wisely: Rifles are king here.
  • Use A Hall control: Puts pressure on both A and Mid.
  • Play for post-plant lineups: Wide sites allow safe utility usage from afar.
IN ROTATION

Corrode

Corrode is the twelfth map, located in Mont-Saint-Michel, France. It is a traditional two-site three-lane map with no dynamic elements, focusing on fundamental gameplay.

Important Callouts

  • A Site – A Main, A Yard, A Elbow, A Site, A Link
  • Mid – Mid Stairs, Mid Bottom, Mid Top, Mid Window
  • B Site – B Main, B Site, B Lobby, B Link, B Elbow, B Tower, B Arch

Pro Tips

  • Prioritize mid control: Connects quickly to both sites.
  • Abuse wallbangs: Many surfaces are penetrable.
  • Play rat corners: Tight angles allow for surprise plays.
OUT OF ROTATION

Fracture

Fracture is located in Santa Fe, New Mexico. Its H-shape layout splits the map, with Defenders spawning in the center. Attackers can pinch sites from opposite sides using zip lines.

Important Callouts

  • A Site – A Main, A Hall, A Rope, A Drop, A Site, A Dish
  • B Site – B Main, B Tower, B Canteen, B Site, B Arcade, B Tunnel, B Tree

Pro Tips

  • Hit sites from both sides: Overwhelm defenders with a pincer attack.
  • Use ziplines for fast pressure: Silent during buy phase.
  • Play around the four orbs: Gain ultimates faster.
IN ROTATION

Haven

Haven is located in Thimphu, Bhutan. It is unique for having three spike sites (A, B, C). Mid doors have destructible panels that can be broken to allow wallbangs.

Important Callouts

  • A Site – A Long, A Short, A Heaven, A Hell, A Site
  • B Site – B Main, B Site, B Backsite
  • C Site – C Long, C Garage, C Platform, C Site

Pro Tips

  • Use the three-site advantage: Spread defense thin.
  • Break Mid door panels early: Deny safe rotations.
  • Control Garage and Mid: Key for rotations.
OUT OF ROTATION

Icebox

Icebox is located in Bennett Island, Russia. It features horizontal ziplines and vertical combat spaces. Sites are multi-level with nests where the spike can be planted.

Important Callouts

  • A Site – A Belt, A Nest, A Rafters, A Screens, A Site
  • Mid – Mid Blue, Mid Tube
  • B Site – B Main, B Yellow, B Green, B Snowman, B Site

Pro Tips

  • Abuse verticality: Use high ground for off-angles.
  • Use utility to clear tight angles.
  • Control B Main and Yellow: Essential for B site executes.
OUT OF ROTATION

Lotus

Lotus is located in Western Ghats, India. It is the second three-site map. It features rotating doors, a silent drop, and a destructible wall. This map is Nikko’s personal favorite!

Important Callouts

  • A Site – A Main, A Tree, A Top, A Drop, A Site, A Link
  • B Site – B Main, B Pillar, B Site
  • C Site – C Mound, C Main, C Waterfall, C Site

Pro Tips

  • Use rotating doors for fakes and rotations.
  • Control C Mound early: Key space to contest.
  • Break the A Main wall strategically: Opens new paths.
IN ROTATION

Pearl

Pearl is located in Lisbon, Portugal. It is an underwater geo-dome map with no dynamic mechanics, focusing on a traditional three-lane layout.

Important Callouts

  • A Site – A Main, A Secret, A Flowers, A Dugout, A Site
  • Mid – Mid Plaza, Mid Connector, Mid Doors, Mid Shops
  • B Site – B Main, B Link, B Tower, B Site

Pro Tips

  • Control Mid early: Mid gives access to both sites.
  • Use A Secret for splits: Pressures A Site from multiple angles.
  • Clear close corners carefully: Many tight angles.
IN ROTATION

Split

Split is located in Tokyo, Japan. It features ascenders (ropes) that allow vertical movement in key areas like Mid and sites.

Important Callouts

  • A Site – A Main, A Ramps, A Screens, A Heaven, A Site
  • Mid – Mid Mail, Mid Vent, Mid Bottom, Mid Top
  • B Site – B Main, B Garage, B Heaven, B Site

Pro Tips

  • Fight for Mid control: Opens up the map for attackers.
  • Use vertical utility: Clear Heaven and Ramps.
  • Play off close angles: Shotguns and SMGs can be strong here.
IN ROTATION

Sunset

Sunset is located in Los Angeles, USA. It is a traditional map with a focus on mid control. It features a mechanical door between B Market and Mid.

Important Callouts

  • A Site – A Main, A Lobby, A Tower, A Site, A Backsite
  • Mid – Mid Courtyard, Mid Top, Mid Bottom, Mid Doors
  • B Site – B Main, B Market, B Site, B Backsite

Pro Tips

  • Control Mid Courtyard: Allows fast rotations.
  • Play around the Market door: Isolate enemies.
  • Use utility to clear tight angles.
